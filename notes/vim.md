Open: vim fileName

2 modes: normal and insert

Insert mode:
  ^n, ^y

normal mode:
  Going to insert mode:
    a, A, i, I, o, O
  Navigation:
    w, W, b, B, $, 0, {}, /__, *, #, n, N, hjkl
  Edit: 
    dd, yy, p, P, di_, da_, ci_, ca_

exit:
  :q, :q!, ZZ
